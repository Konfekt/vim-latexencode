LatexEncode, UTF-8 to LaTeX conversion        *LatexEncode* *latexencode*

This Vim plug-in provides a command *:LatexEncode* that operates on a range (by
default the whole buffer) and converts UTF-8 characters to LaTeX commands
using pylatexenc available at https://github.com/phfaist/pylatexenc .

vim:tw=78:ts=2:sts=2:sw=2:ft=help:norl
