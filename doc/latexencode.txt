LatexEncode, UTF-8 to LaTeX conversion        *LatexEncode* *latexencode*

This Vim plug-in provides the commands `:LatexEncode` and `:Latex2Text` that
operate on a range (by default the whole buffer) and convert UTF-8 characters
to LaTeX commands (and vice-versa) using pylatexenc available at
https://github.com/phfaist/pylatexenc.
